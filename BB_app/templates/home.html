About Us

PulsePoint is a digital platform connecting donors, patients, and hospitals. We aim to simplify the process of blood donation and requests, ensuring that every drop counts and reaches those who need it most.

Our mission is to create a seamless, trustworthy, and accessible ecosystem for blood donation. We believe that saving lives should be simple, quick, and efficient, and we strive to bridge the gap between those willing to help and those in urgent need.

PulsePoint empowers donors by providing timely notifications about blood requirements in nearby hospitals and communities. For patients and hospitals, our platform ensures that critical blood needs are met promptly, reducing delays and improving patient outcomes.

Guided by values of compassion, transparency, and community service, we are committed to making blood donation more than just an act – we aim to make it a life-saving movement. Join us in our mission and become a part of a caring network that touches countless lives every day.

Our Services

Donor Registration

Register as a donor and help save lives by donating blood safely and regularly.

Blood Requests

Patients can request the required blood type and connect with nearby hospitals.

Hospital Partnership

Hospitals can manage their blood stock and fulfill patient needs efficiently.

Health Tips

Community Support
