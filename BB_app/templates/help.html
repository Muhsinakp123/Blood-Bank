Help & Support

Welcome to the Help & Support Center! Whether you are a donor, patient, or hospital staff, we are here to guide you. Our platform ensures safe and easy blood donation and request management. Browse the sections below to get assistance.

FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about eligibility, donation frequency, and preparation before donating blood.

Email Support

Contact our support team at support@lifelink.org. Expect responses within 24 hours.

Live Chat

Chat with our support staff for real-time assistance on donor registration, blood requests, and hospital coordination.

Step-by-Step Guides

For Donors:

  1. Register on the platform with your details.
  2. Check upcoming donation camps or nearby hospitals.
  3. Schedule your donation appointment.
  4. Follow pre-donation and post-donation guidelines.

For Patients:

  1. Create a request specifying blood group, quantity, and urgency.
  2. Connect with nearby donors or hospitals.
  3. Track response and confirm availability.

For Hospitals:

  1. Register your hospital and blood bank.
  2. Update blood stock regularly.
  3. Respond to patient requests and notify donors if needed.

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Unable to register: Check that all fields are filled and email is valid.
  • Cannot find donors: Verify location settings and blood group selection.
  • Hospital not responding: Contact support via email or live chat.
  • Login issues: Use password recovery or contact support.

Emergency Contacts & Blood Bank Resources

In case of urgent blood requirements, reach out to the following:

  • Red Cross Helpline: +91 12345 67890
  • Local Blood Banks: Visit NACO India
  • Hospital Emergency Departments for immediate needs.
